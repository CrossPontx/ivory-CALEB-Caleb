## About Ivory's Choice

### What is Ivory's Choice?

Ivory's Choice is an AI-powered nail design platform that connects clients with professional nail technicians. The app allows users to:

- **Generate custom nail designs** using AI technology
- **Capture photos** of their hands and visualize designs on them
- **Browse and save** nail design inspiration
- **Book appointments** with professional nail technicians
- **Share designs** with friends and nail techs
- **Build portfolios** (for nail techs)

### Key Features

**For Clients:**
- AI-powered nail design generation
- Photo capture and design visualization
- Design collections and favorites
- Nail tech search and booking
- Design sharing capabilities

**For Nail Technicians:**
- Professional profile and portfolio
- Client booking management
- AI design breakdown tool
- Stripe Connect wallet for payouts
- Client communication tools
- Business analytics

### Platform Availability

- **iOS App:** Available on App Store (native app with Apple Watch support)
- **Web App:** Accessible via web browser at https://ivoryschoice.com
- **Android:** Coming soon

### AI Design Breakdown Tool

**What It Does:**

The AI Design Breakdown analyzes any nail design image and provides:
- Difficulty level (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
- Estimated time to complete
- Products and tools needed
- Specific techniques required
- Step-by-step instructions
- Pro tips and tricks
- Common mistakes to avoid

**How to Use:**

1. Open a booking with attached design
2. Tap **Get Design Breakdown**
3. AI analyzes the design (uses GPT-4 Vision)
4. Receive detailed breakdown in seconds
5. Review instructions before appointment
6. Save breakdown for reference

**Breakdown Includes:**

- **Preparation:** Base coat, nail prep steps
- **Color Application:** Specific colors, layering
- **Design Technique:** Freehand, stamping, chrome, etc.
- **Tools Needed:** Dotting tool, striping brush, etc.
- **Products:** Gel polish brands, builder gel, top coat
- **Step-by-Step:** Detailed instructions for each step
- **Curing Times:** If using gel/UV
- **Pro Tips:** Expert advice for best results
- **Common Mistakes:** What to avoid

**Professional Terminology:**

The AI uses nail tech language you understand:
- **Cure:** Harden gel polish under UV/LED lamp
- **Float:** Apply color without touching cuticle
- **Encapsulate:** Seal design with clear gel
- **Cap the free edge:** Apply product to tip edge
- **Flash cure:** Quick 5-10 second cure
- **Wipe off tacky layer:** Remove sticky residue

## Booking System

### How Booking Works

**Client Side:**

1. Client searches for nail techs
2. Views your profile and services
3. Selects service
4. Chooses date and time
5. Optionally attaches design
6. Adds special notes
7. Submits booking request
8. Pays via Apple IAP (iOS) or Stripe (web)

**Tech Side:**

1. Receive booking notification
2. Review request details
3. Accept or decline
4. If accepted, appointment confirmed
5. Receive payment after completion

### Booking Statuses

- **Pending:** Awaiting tech confirmation
- **Confirmed:** Tech accepted, appointment scheduled
- **Completed:** Service finished
- **Cancelled:** Booking cancelled by client or tech

### Cancellation Policy

**Client Cancellations:**

- Can cancel anytime before appointment
- Refund policy depends on timing:
  - 24+ hours before: Full refund
  - Less than 24 hours: No refund (tech's discretion)
- Cancellation fee may apply

**Tech Cancellations:**

- Should cancel as early as possible
- Client receives full refund
- Frequent cancellations affect your rating
- May impact search ranking

### Reviews and Ratings

**After Completed Booking:**

- Client can leave review
- Rate 1-5 stars

- Reviews appear on your profile

**Responding to Reviews:**

- Can respond to client reviews
- Be professional and courteous

**Review Guidelines:**

1. Check internet connection

### AI Generation Issues

**Design Generation Failed:**

**Possible Causes:**
- Insufficient credits
- Poor internet connection
- Server temporarily down
- Invalid prompt or image

**Solutions:**
1. Check credit balance
2. Verify internet connection
3. Try simpler prompt
4. Use different photo
5. Wait a few minutes and retry

**Design Quality Issues:**

- Use high-quality hand photos
- Ensure good lighting
- Keep hand in frame
- Avoid blurry images
- Be specific in prompts

**Generation Taking Too Long:**

- Normal time: 2-5 seconds
- If longer than 30 seconds, refresh
- Check internet speed
- Try during off-peak hours

1. Check file size (max 10MB)

3. Check camera permissions
4. Try different photo
5. Compress large images

**Photo Not Appearing:**

1. Wait for upload to complete
2. Check internet connection
3. Refresh the page
4. Try uploading again
5. Contact support with error message
