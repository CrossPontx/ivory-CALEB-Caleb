# Ivory's Choice Revenue Model

## Overview
Separate pricing tiers for regular users (clients) and nail technicians, optimized for each user type's needs.

## Client Pricing

### Free Tier
- **Cost:** $0
- **Includes:**
  - 5 signup credits
  - Basic design tools
  - Community support
  - Browse nail techs
  - Book appointments

### Pro Tier
- **Cost:** $20/month
- **Includes:**
  - 20 AI design credits per month
  - Buy additional credits anytime
  - Priority support
  - Advanced design tools
  - Save favorite designs
  - Share with nail techs
  - Credits roll over (never expire)

### Credit Packages (Pro users only)
- 5 credits - $4.99
- 10 credits - $8.99 (10% savings)
- 25 credits - $19.99 (20% savings)
- 50 credits - $34.99 (30% savings)
- 100 credits - $59.99 (40% savings)

## Nail Tech Pricing

### Free Tier
- **Cost:** $0
- **Includes:**
  - 5 free bookings
  - Basic profile
  - Portfolio showcase
  - Client messaging
  - Stripe Connect payouts

### Business Tier
- **Cost:** $60/month
- **Required after:** 5 bookings
- **Includes:**
  - Unlimited bookings
  - Priority listing in search
  - Advanced analytics
  - Client management tools
  - Custom branding
  - Premium support
  - Featured placement

## Revenue Breakdown

### From Clients
- **Subscriptions:** $20/month recurring
- **Credit purchases:** $4.99 - $59.99 one-time
- **Target:** Design enthusiasts, frequent users

### From Nail Techs
- **Subscriptions:** $60/month recurring
- **Booking fees:** Stripe Connect (standard rates)
- **Target:** Professional nail technicians

### Booking Transactions
- **Platform fee:** Included in tech subscription
- **Payment processing:** Stripe Connect (2.9% + $0.30)
- **Payout:** Direct to tech's bank account

## Platform Economics

### Apple IAP (iOS)
- **Apple's cut:** 30% (15% after year 1 for subscriptions)
- **Client Pro:** $20/month → $14 to platform (year 1)
- **Tech Business:** $60/month → $42 to platform (year 1)

### Stripe (Web)
- **Stripe's cut:** 2.9% + $0.30
- **Client Pro:** $20/month → $19.42 to platform
- **Tech Business:** $60/month → $58.26 to platform

### Booking Payments
- **Processed via:** Stripe Connect
- **Platform fee:** Included in tech subscription
- **Tech receives:** Full amount minus Stripe fees

## Growth Strategy

### Client Acquisition
1. Free tier with 5 credits
2. Showcase AI design quality
3. Upsell to Pro for more credits
4. Referral bonuses

### Tech Acquisition
1. 5 free bookings to prove value
2. Show booking volume potential
3. Require subscription after 5 bookings
4. Premium features justify $60/month

### Retention
- Credits never expire (clients)
- Unlimited bookings (techs)
- Continuous feature improvements
- Community building
